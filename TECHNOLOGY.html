Academy

This 10-month ICT learnership provides unemployed youth with Cisco international qualifications in Web and Applications Development or Networking & Security. The ICT Learnership takes the form of a bootcamp where students are placed in a high pressurized, fast-paced learning environment. The aim hereof is to simulate a typical IT support, working environment as part of preparing students for employment. The technical training is supplemented with essential life skills workshops such as goal setting and motivation, financial literacy, job readiness, \ self-awareness, etc. Students are prepared for the workplace through training, support and mentorship to maximize job readiness and secure placement.
